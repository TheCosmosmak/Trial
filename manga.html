The Last Signal

In the year 2242, the megastructure known as 'The Spire' was all that remained of the old world's ambition.

A lone scavenger, Ren, sifted through the digital ghosts of the past.

"They say it's a message from before the Silence. A warning... or a welcome."

The signal wasn't a warning. It was a simple, repeating loop of data: the complete genetic code for Earth's lost flora. A digital seedbank.

Ren looked out at the grey expanse. He didn't find a warning. He found a blueprint for a new world.
