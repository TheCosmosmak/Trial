A Journey Through Time // 歴史

The Stone Age | ~2.5M - 3000 BCE

Humanity's dawn. The invention of tools, the control of fire, and the birth of language and art lay the foundation for all that follows.

Ancient Civilizations | ~3000 BCE - 500 CE

Empires rise and fall. Writing, mathematics, philosophy, and law emerge, structuring society.

The Industrial Revolution | ~1760 - 1840

Steam power and machinery transform the world. Mass production, urbanization, and global trade create a new, interconnected economic landscape.

The Information Age | ~1970 - Present

The digital revolution. Computers, the internet, and mobile technology connect the globe, making information instantly accessible to all.

The Symbiotic Age | The Future

Humanity begins to merge with artificial intelligence, creating a future of unprecedented creativity, discovery, and new frontiers of existence.
